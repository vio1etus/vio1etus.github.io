About Me

I hold a Ph.D. in Cyberspace Security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research centers on two complementary directions: Trustworthy AI and AI for safety-critical applications.

For Trustworthy AI, I develop trustworthy AI methods to enhance the robustness, security, privacy, and reliability of AI systems under heterogeneous and dynamic real-world conditions, leveraging robust learning, anomaly detection, distributed/federated learning, privacy-preserving techniques (e.g., differential privacy), and optimization methods.

For AI in safety-critical applications, I develop data-driven and AI-enabled solutions to enable safe, reliable, and trustworthy AI deployment in domains such as healthcare, wireless networks, IoT, and cybersecurity, leveraging domain-aware modeling, machine learning, data analytics, and system-level optimization techniques.
